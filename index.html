About Me

Bio

I am currently a Computer Science student at Florida Polytechnic University, and I am looking for internship opportunities. I am experienced in game development, graphic design, photography, programming, and video editing. I would like to bring these talents to your organization and kickstart a career in Software Development!

Interests

My main interests are photography, graphic design, and game development. I enjoy creating things, and game development specifically is alluring because it combines so many of my interests and skills into one product. I frequently develop my own film, and many of the photographs found on the Gallery page were developed by myself. Graphic design is specifically interesting as it allows me to use my photographic work in a different context than it may have been originally intended for.

Experience

I am experienced in game development, graphic design, photography, programming, and video editing. I have competed in many game jams both by myself and while working in a team. You can view some of the results of these jams above, and you can see some of my photographic and graphic design work on the Gallery page.
